    • Doctor Consultation at Bliss Healthcare

    Doctor consultation services are an essential part of healthcare, providing patients with access to medical professionals for advice, diagnosis, and treatment. In Kenya, there are several options available for patients seeking doctor consultation services, including traditional in-person appointments and virtual consultation

    Accessibility

    Online doctor consultation services in Kenya provide access to medical professionals who may not be available in a patient's immediate vicinity. This is especially beneficial for patients living in remote areas or those with mobility issues who may struggle to travel to a medical facility. Online consultations can be done from the comfort of a patient's home or office, making healthcare more accessible to all.

    Time-Saving

    Online doctor consultation services in Kenya eliminate the need for patients to physically travel to a medical facility, saving them time and money on transportation. Patients can schedule virtual consultations at a time that is convenient for them, reducing wait times and allowing for more efficient use of time.

    Cost-Effective

    Online doctor consultation services in Kenya are often more cost-effective than traditional in-person appointments. Patients can save money on transportation, parking fees, and other expenses associated with visiting a medical facility. Virtual consultations are also often less expensive than in-person appointments, making healthcare more affordable for all.

    Confidentiality

    Online doctor consultation services in Kenya provide a level of privacy and confidentiality that may not be possible in traditional in-person appointments. Patients can speak with their doctor from the comfort of their own home, eliminating the need to disclose personal information to strangers in a waiting room.

    Introducing a new healing process

    Medications and treatments that are prescribed are most effective when the patient believes that the particular medication or treatment is going to help their illness. This belief is generated when there has been trust developed between the patient and the doctor providing the treatments. Also, the benefits of treatment have been adequately explained by the physician and, most importantly, that the physician believes that the treatment is going to be effective in most cases. Establishment of follow-up visits soon after the initial visit is extremely important in the healing process. It allows the physician the time and opportunity to review with the patient any new symptoms, test results, response to treatment, and any need for changes in diagnosis or treatment. Most importantly, it will stress the fact that the physician is interested in the progress and recovery of the patient. Frequent visits are necessary as part of the healing until the patient’s condition is stabilized..
